Selvala, Heart of the Wilds

Overview

Selvala is one of the strongest and most consistent decks I have constructed for the commander format. The deck features many strong creature based strategies and value engines in order to grind out victories against multiple opponents.

One such value engine is an ingrained ramp and card draw strategy based around selvala herself with the aim being to play creatures in ascending order of power to maximise your card drawing capabilities as well as build up your generation of mana with her second ability.

Strategy

The deck features multiple ways of deploying these creatures without paying for them or at least accelerating them out ahead of the expected turn order. These include cards such as Selvala's Stampede which allows me to either get permanents from my hand, or the next creature from my library, both of which are very favourable outcomes.

Example Cards

These cards are an example of the types of cards included in the deck.
Image Role Description
Accelleration

Sol Ring is an artifact which accelerates the deck's ability to play creatures significantly by increasing the amount of mana we have available by 2, this enables the deck to play 4 mana spells on turn 2. This also allows the deck to put the commander in play on turn two which is extremely important for fast matchups.

Sweeper/Wrath

All is Dust is a powerful card which enables the deck to clear the board of most threats and reset all players back to the same playing level. This card has been included as a way for the deck to catch up once other players have already developed a powerful board state.

The inclusion of such a powerful sweeper effect is a little unusual for this kind of deck as generally speaking creature based strategies are weak towards such effects. However after playing the deck without any sort of effect like this I found that there were many games where I wanted to remove a lot of threats at once and spot removal wasn't going to cut it.
